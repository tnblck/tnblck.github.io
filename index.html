How I
work

work

With meticulous attention to detail, I bring my graphic design expertise from the reputable School of Visual Arts to each project. Recognized for my distinctive performance in the entrance examination, I had the privilege of being sponsored by Pierre Bergé and graduated as valedictorian. Demonstrating my capabilities, I created a self-produced musical 3D animation that received early recognition. Drawing inspiration from my synesthesia, I infuse my designs with vibrant colors and captivating shapes, harmonizing the visual and auditory realms. Alongside my interest in VFX, I integrate cinematic storytelling techniques to create visually compelling and immersive designs. Taking a holistic approach, I conduct thorough market research and employ diverse media to create impactful brand identities.
